Choropleth Leaflet Map

Above is a Choropleth Map displaying homeless rates in the US in 2014. It contains an interactive information box that shows the percentage as you hover over each state. I chose to depict the data with the color purple because that is the color of the homeless awareness ribbon. Homeless data was obtained through data.gov and population data from the US Census Bureau. Population data for 2014 is an estimate. Homeless counts conducted through Point-In-Time (PIT) and Housing Inventory (HIC) counts.
